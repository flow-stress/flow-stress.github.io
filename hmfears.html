HOW TO MANAGE FEAR INDUCED STRESS

Lions, tigers, and bears, oh my! Here are some ways to minimize the negative impacts of fear-induced stress.

-- Gradually begin to introduce facing your fears regularly.

-- If you find that you can't tolerate self-exposure, ask a friend!

-- Discover what coping mechanisms work for you (reading, writing, etc.).

-- Take things slowly. No pressure, any progress is good progress!
